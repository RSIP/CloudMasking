Cloud Masking

CloudMasking is a Qgis plugin for make the masking of clouds, cloud shadow, cirrus, aerosols, ice/snow and water for Landsat (4, 5, 7 and 8) products using different process such as Fmask, Blue Band, Cloud QA and Pixel QA.

SMByC

Group of Forest and Carbon Monitoring System (SMByC by its acronym in Spanish), operated by the Institute of Hydrology, Meteorology and Environmental Studies (IDEAM), Colombia.
